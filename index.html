KENYAN COFFEE

Kenyan coffee is renowned for its exceptional quality and distinctive taste. Here are some compelling reasons why someone might choose to buy Kenyan coffee: Full-Bodied Flavor: Kenyan coffee is known for its full-bodied acidity and rich berry flavors, offering a vibrant and complex taste experience. 1. High Altitude Growth: The coffee grows at high altitudes in the Kenyan highlands, which contributes to the beans’ unique flavor profile. 2. Quality Assurance: The cooperative production system in Kenya ensures a high standard of quality, with coffee sold through well-regulated auctions. 3. Unique Varieties: Kenya produces famous coffee varieties like SL-28 and SL-34, developed for their high yields and excellent quality. 4. Washed Processing: High-quality Kenyan coffee undergoes washed processing, which enhances its clean, bright flavour
