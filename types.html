We classify our coffees into 8 categories:

Kenya E (Elephant Bean)

The largest beans, often peaberries, where one coffee seed grows alone in the cherry.

Kenya PB (Peaberry)

Beans with a single round coffee bean per cherry, rather than the usual two half-beans.

Kenya AA

Considered one of the finest specialty coffees worldwide, grown at high elevations with bright acidity and often exhibiting floral and fruity notes.

Kenya AB

A mix of A and B beans, smaller than AA.

Kenya C

Smaller beans than AB, often from lower altitudes.

Kenya TT

Lighter beans sorted out from AA and AB.

Kenya T

Smaller, often broken beans or fragments.

Kenya MH/ML

Beans with slight defects or irregular sizes.
